About

Documenting the relationship between places in the midst of great change and the people that inhabit them, this is the genre I work within. Working in both the digital and film, color and black and white, my images are made and presented in the method best suited towards the project I am currently working on. With a clear story and narrative I want to present, I take off by car, feet, or plane. But always allowing my curiosity and instinct to take me off towards interesting sights and sounds.
